The Great Realignment: Navigating Global Monetary Policy

Published: Feb 24, 2025 • Dr. Elizabeth Montgomery
Global markets are entering a "Neutral Era." Our analysis indicates that while inflation is cooling, the cost of capital will remain structurally higher than the previous decade.

The global economic landscape of 2025 is fundamentally different from the post-pandemic recovery era. We are witnessing what experts call "The Great Realignment"—a period where the cost of capital returns to historical norms while technological innovation drives productivity at an exponential pace.

1. Interest Rate Projections

The expectation of a rapid return to zero-interest rates is unfounded. The structural shifts in labor markets and global energy transitions have created a higher floor for core inflation. Central banks are now targeting a "Neutral Rate" that balances growth without re-igniting price surges.

2. AI in Financial Infrastructure

Artificial Intelligence is giờ đây là cốt lõi của quản lý rủi ro. Các mô hình máy học có khả năng xử lý tâm lý địa chính trị trong mili giây, tạo ra một môi trường thị trường nơi sự bất cân xứng thông tin bị thu hẹp đáng kể.

Conclusion

The path forward requires a sophisticated understanding of macro fundamentals. The 2025 outlook remains cautiously optimistic, provided market participants adapt to the rapid digitalization of financial architecture.
